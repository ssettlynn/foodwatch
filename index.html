The Scale

Three tiers, defined by the UN FAO

Every country-year is scored on official undernourishment bands.

Low

Undernourishment below 5%. Food access broadly secure; monitoring continues.

Medium

5–15%. Vulnerable to shocks — the tier where early action is cheapest and most effective.
