The Scale

Three tiers, defined by the UN FAO

Every country-year is scored on official undernourishment bands.

Low
<5%

Undernourishment below 5%. Food access broadly secure; monitoring continues.

Medium
5–15%

Vulnerable to shocks — the tier where early action is cheapest and most effective.

High
15%+

Above 15%. Concentrated in Sub-Saharan Africa and crisis-affected states.
